# Privacy Policy

**KlarText** is a plain text editor for macOS.

KlarText does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reporting sent to third parties
- No network connections of any kind
- All preferences are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS UserDefaults

The only data KlarText accesses is the text files you explicitly open or create. That data never leaves your device.
